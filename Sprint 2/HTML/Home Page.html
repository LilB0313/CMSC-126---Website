Escape to your own private sanctuary in Agahay Guesthouse Resort, located at Maribojoc, Philippines. Situated on the serene mountains of western Bohol, this homey resort is an exclusive 5-star resort placing emphasis on your comfort and privacy.


The Agahay Guesthouse Resort is best for its homey feels and privacy. Eco-friendly principles and energy conservation measures are in place to ensure sustainable tourism. Rest assured that you will have the time of your life at Agahay Guesthouse Resort.
